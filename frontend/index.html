Our Story

Founded in 2005, Filmax Jambo Tours has been at the forefront of African wildlife tourism for nearly two decades. Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the breathtaking beauty of Africa's wilderness with the world while promoting sustainable tourism practices.

We believe that a safari should be more than just a vacation - it should be a transformative experience that connects you with nature, culture, and yourself. Our team of expert guides, conservationists, and hospitality professionals work tirelessly to create journeys that exceed expectations.

From the vast plains of the Serengeti to the dense forests of Uganda, we've curated experiences that showcase Africa's incredible diversity while supporting local communities and conservation efforts.
